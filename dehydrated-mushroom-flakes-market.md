<p><strong>The Dehydrated Mushroom Flakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehydrated mushroom flakes market primarily consists of two types: air drying and freeze drying. Air drying involves exposing mushrooms to warm air, removing moisture while retaining flavor and texture, making it a cost-effective method. In contrast, freeze drying preserves more nutrients and flavor by rapidly freezing the mushrooms and then removing moisture in a vacuum, resulting in a lightweight product with a longer shelf life. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences and applications in culinary and food processing industries.</p></p>
