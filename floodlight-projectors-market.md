<p><strong>The Floodlight Projectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-halide</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floodlight projectors are categorized primarily into two types: metal-halide and LED. Metal-halide projectors utilize gas-discharge technology, offering bright illumination and extensive color rendering, making them popular for sports and large venues. However, they consume more energy and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, LED floodlights are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and provide instant brightness with minimal heat output. Their versatility and lower maintenance costs are driving their adoption across various applications, including outdoor events, architectural lighting, and security purposes.</p></p>
