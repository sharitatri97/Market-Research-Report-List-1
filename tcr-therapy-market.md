<p><strong>The TCR Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transduction of Chimeric Antigen Receptor</li><li>Transduction of TCR α/β Heterodimer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TCR therapy market comprises two primary types: transduction of chimeric antigen receptors (CARs) and transduction of TCR α/β heterodimers. CAR transduction involves modifying T cells to express receptors targeting specific tumor antigens, enhancing their ability to identify and destroy cancer cells. Conversely, TCR α/β heterodimer transduction focuses on equipping T cells with receptors that recognize peptide-antigen complexes presented by major histocompatibility complex molecules, allowing for a broader range of tumor recognition and immune response activation.</p></p>
