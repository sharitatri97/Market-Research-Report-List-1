<p><p>The AiP (Antenna in Package) module market encompasses various technologies, primarily LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic), HDI (High-Density Interconnect), and FOWLP (Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging). LTCC offers high performance and excellent thermal stability, making it ideal for RF applications. HDI provides compact design with increased routing capabilities, suitable for high-frequency devices. FOWLP allows for a smaller form factor and improved thermal management, facilitating integration of multiple components in a single package, enhancing overall device performance and reliability.</p></p>
