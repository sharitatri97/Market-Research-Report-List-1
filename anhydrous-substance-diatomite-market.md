<p><p>The anhydrous diatomite market is segmented into three main types: Natural, Calcined, and Flux Calcined. Natural diatomite is untreated and retains its original properties, used primarily for filtration and insulation. Calcined diatomite undergoes heating to enhance its porosity and improve its chemical performance, making it suitable for various industrial applications. Flux Calcined diatomite is subjected to a specific heating process that alters its structure, resulting in lower density and higher reactivity, ideal for specialized applications such as ceramics and agriculture.</p></p>
