<p><p>The eco-friendly green roof market consists of three main types: extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive green roofs. Extensive green roofs are lightweight and require minimal maintenance, featuring shallow soil and drought-resistant plants. Semi-intensive roofs offer a balance, with deeper soil supporting a wider variety of plants, requiring moderate upkeep. Intensive green roofs, akin to gardens, have deep soil that supports diverse vegetation and amenities, demanding significant maintenance. Each type contributes to sustainability, stormwater management, and urban biodiversity in unique ways.</p></p>
