<p><p>The Aircraft Smoke Detection and Fire Extinguishing System market comprises two primary types: heat sensing and smoke sensing. Heat sensing systems detect abnormal temperature rises, alerting crew members to potential fire hazards. These systems are efficient in environments where smoke may not be immediately apparent. In contrast, smoke sensing systems identify particulate matter in the air, providing early warnings of fires. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring aviation safety by enabling prompt detection and response to fire-related threats.</p></p>
