<p><p>The ultra-large volume wearable injectors market is divided into two main types: on-body injectors and off-body injectors. Off-body injectors, on the other hand, are separate devices that can be held or positioned near the injection site, typically requiring manual activation. Both types aim to enhance patient convenience and adherence for large-volume drug delivery.</p></p>
