<p><ul><li>Round Bend U-Bolts</li><li>Square Bend U-Bolts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The U-Bolts market is primarily categorized into two types: Round Bend U-Bolts and Square Bend U-Bolts. Round Bend U-Bolts feature a smooth, curved design ideal for securing cylindrical objects, commonly used in applications like plumbing and automotive. In contrast, Square Bend U-Bolts have sharp edges, providing superior load distribution and stability, making them suitable for various industrial and construction applications. The choice between these designs depends on specific uses and the required strength and adaptability in fastening solutions.</p></p>
