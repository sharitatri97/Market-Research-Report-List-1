<p><strong>The Automotive Silicone Elastomers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV)</li><li>Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV)</li><li>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR)</li><li>Fluorosilicone Rubber (FSR)</li><li>High Consistency Silicone Rubber (HCR)</li><li>Silicone Gels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive silicone elastomers market encompasses several types tailored for various applications. High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV) silicone is ideal for high-heat environments, while Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV) silicone cures at ambient temperatures, suitable for sealants and gaskets. Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) offers excellent flow characteristics for complex mold designs. Fluorosilicone Rubber (FSR) provides enhanced chemical resistance. High Consistency Silicone Rubber (HCR) is dense and durable for parts like hoses. Silicone gels are flexible and cushioning, used in vibration dampening and insulation.</p></p>
