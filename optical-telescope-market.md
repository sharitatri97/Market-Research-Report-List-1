<p><ul><li>Refracting telescope</li><li>Reflector Telescope</li><li>Catadioptric telescope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical telescope market comprises three main types: refracting telescopes, which use lenses to focus light and are known for their clarity; reflector telescopes, employing mirrors to gather and focus light, offering larger apertures at lower costs; and catadioptric telescopes, combining lenses and mirrors for compact designs and versatile performance. Each type caters to varying user needs and preferences, influencing market trends. Refractors appeal to beginners, reflectors attract amateur astronomers, while catadioptrics are popular for their portability and adaptability.</p></p>
