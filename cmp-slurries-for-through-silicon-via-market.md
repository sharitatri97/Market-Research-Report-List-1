<p><p>CMP slurries for through silicon via (TSV) applications are crucial for achieving high-quality surface polishing during semiconductor manufacturing. Front side slurries are utilized for polishing the front side of silicon wafers, ensuring smoothness and preparing surfaces for microfabrication. In contrast, back side slurries focus on the rear side, facilitating vias' formation and enhancing chip performance. Both types are essential for achieving optimal planarization and improving the electrical performance of interconnected silicon chips within advanced packaging technologies.</p></p>
