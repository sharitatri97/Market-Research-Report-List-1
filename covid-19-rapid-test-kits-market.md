<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 rapid test kits market includes several types of diagnostic methods. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) provide quick results using antigen or antibody detection, enabling swift screening. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) tests are more sensitive and quantitative, often used in laboratories for comprehensive analysis of antibodies. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus, providing valuable information about immune response and vaccine efficacy. Each type serves a unique purpose in diagnosing and understanding COVID-19.</p></p>
